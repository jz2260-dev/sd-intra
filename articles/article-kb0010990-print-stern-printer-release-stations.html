Print@Stern / PaperCut: Locating a Printer and Release Station

Print@Stern printers are located throughout NYU Stern buildings. Undergraduate printers are primarily in Tisch Hall. Graduate printers are in Tisch Hall, Kaufman Management Center, and Shimkin Hall.

Support Workflow

Internal step path adapted from the source article.

  1. Ask whether the user needs an undergraduate or graduate printing location.
  2. For undergraduate printer locations, use this list:
    • LC-0A, LC Alcove of Tisch Hall: 2 printers.
    • LC Stair Y, outside LC-19: 1 printer.
    • UC-05A, Tisch Hall: 1 printer.
    • Class of 2008 Lounge, UC level, Tisch Hall: 3 printers, color printing available.
    • K1-75, hallway between KMC and Tisch Hall: 1 printer.
    • T305, UGRAD Meeting Rooms Suite: 2 printers, color printing available.
    • T415A, outside T415: 1 printer.
  3. For graduate printer locations, use this list:
    • LC-0A, LC Alcove of Tisch Hall: 2 printers.
    • UC-05A, Tisch Hall: 1 printer.
    • Class of 2008 Lounge, UC level, Tisch Hall: 3 printers, color printing available.
    • K1-75, hallway between KMC and Tisch Hall: 1 printer.
    • S2-110, MBA Meeting Rooms: 1 printer.
    • K3-81, Rotunda Lounge: 2 printers, color printing available.
    • K4-91, Rotunda Lounge: 2 printers.
    • K4-70, MBA Student Lounge: 1 printer.
    • S4-130, MBA Meeting Rooms Suite: 1 printer, color printing available.
    • K6-84, MBA Student Leader Suite: 1 printer.
  4. If the user already submitted a job, remind them to release it at the selected printer or release station.
